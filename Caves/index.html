Player's Guide

Movement: Use W, A, S, D, Arrow Keys, or Numpad (supports diagonals).

Rest: Press R to rest and recover Health and Stamina. Don't do this while starving!

Inventory: Press I to open your inventory.

  • Press 1-9 to use or equip an item.
  • Press D to toggle Drop Mode.

Magic & Skills: Press B for Spells, K for Weapon Skills.

Chat: Click the chat box and press Enter to send messages.

Exploration: Look for landmarks like signposts (☗), castles (🏰), caves (⛰), and even secret passages!
