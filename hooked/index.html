About Emmy (EmMoMiMi)

Hi! I'm Emmy, the hands and heart behind Hooked by Emmy. What started as a relaxing hobby has blossomed into a passion for creating beautiful, functional, and cozy crochet items. Every piece is made with meticulous care and a whole lot of love right here in Williamsburg, VA.

I believe in the magic of handmade items to bring a little bit of joy into everyday life. Thank you for supporting my small business and dream!
