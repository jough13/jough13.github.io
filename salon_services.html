Posh Salon: User Manual

Welcome! This tool helps you quickly see which stylists perform which services, their prices, and any special notes.

The Basics: Viewing the Matrix

  • Rows & Columns: Each row is a stylist, and each column is a service.
  • Checkboxes (‚úÖ): A checked box means the stylist in that row performs the service in that column.
  • Prices: The price shown is what that specific stylist charges for that service.
  • Note Icons: A small colored square in the corner of a cell means there's a special note attached. Just click the square to read it.

Adding & Removing

All the main controls are located in the button panel at the top of the page.

  • To Add a New Stylist: Type a name into the "New Stylist Name..." box and click the Add Stylist button.
  • To Add a New Service: Type a name into the "New Service Name..." box and click the Add Service button.
  • To Remove a Stylist or Service: Click the trash can icon (üóëÔ∏è) next to its name and confirm your choice.

Editing Information

You can edit almost everything directly in the table!

  • To Edit a Name: Simply click on any stylist or service name. It will turn into an input box. Type the new name and press Enter or click away to save.
  • To Change a Stylist's Level: Click the dropdown menu located directly under the stylist's name.
  • To Change a Price: Click on any price (or on the "Add Price" text). It will turn into an input box.

Using Sticky Notes

  • To Add a Note: (1) Click the Add Note button. (2) Click a color from the palette that appears. (3) Your cursor becomes a `‚ûï`. Click on any square in the table. (4) Type your message and click OK.
  • To View a Note: Click the colored square in the corner of any cell.
  • To Close the Note Popup: Click the '‚úñ' in the top-right corner of the popup window.
  • To Delete a Note: Open the note, then click the "Delete this note" text link at the bottom.

Saving & Managing Your Data

  • Save: Click this to save your work to the current browser. It will be here when you return on the same computer.
  • Export: Click this to save a backup file (`salon-data.json`) to your computer. Use this for permanent backups or sharing.
  • Import: Click this to load data from an exported file. This will completely replace the current matrix.
