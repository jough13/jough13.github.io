Economic Primer

DOCUMENT ID: HELIOS-DIRECTORATE-STRATEGY-MEMO-77-GAMMA
SUBJECT: Foundational Principles of Relativistic Economics & Temporal Arbitrage
COMPILED BY: The Office of CEO Alistair Caine (Archived GSY 2550)
CLEARANCE: DIRECTORATE LEVEL - EYES ONLY

I. Abstract: The Economics of Yesterday's News

In a light-speed-limited universe, information is history... To Helios Dynamics, it is a predictive tool. Temporal Arbitrage is the science of using our superior relativistic drive technology to act on "past" events in a timeframe that allows us to control their "future."

II. The Mechanism: A Practical Example

The principle is best illustrated with a classic case study: the acquisition of the independent mining colony on Cygnus X-1's third moon, "Promise."...

III. Applications & Implications

Economic: This is our primary tool for market manipulation, hostile takeovers, and resource acquisition...

The Human Cost (The "Time Ghosts"): This strategy requires a special class of employee... They are permanently disconnected from the normal flow of human history...

IV. Strategic Vulnerabilities

Temporal Arbitrage is our greatest strength, but it relies on predictable history. It is useless against chaotic, non-linear events. The Galactic Archive is the ultimate wildcard...

The Alliance Charter

DOCUMENT ID: FTA-GUIDE-NEW-CAPTAINS-V3.4
SUBJECT: An Introduction to the Principles of the Free-Trader Alliance
COMPILED BY: The Concord of Captains, New Geneva Office
CLEARANCE: FTA MEMBERS - EYES ONLY

I. Foreword: More Than a Guild, Less Than a Nation

Welcome, Captain. You are now part of the oldest and most resilient institution in the human diaspora... The Free-Trader Alliance is a promise. It is a handshake across the void. It is a recognition that out here, in the long, slow dark between worlds, the only things that truly have value are a signed contract, a good reputation, and a ship that will answer your distress call.

II. The Concord of Captains: The First Among Equals

The Alliance has no central ruler. Our leadership is a decentralized council of our most respected and trusted peers, known as The Concord of Captains...

III. The Trust Ledger: The Currency of Reputation

A credit can be forged. A corporate promise can be broken. A reputation on the Trust Ledger is absolute... A high Trust Score is more valuable than a hold full of platinum. It grants you access to the best contracts, lines of credit based on your name alone, and safe harbor in any FTA-aligned port.

IV. Hauler-Clan Culture: The Ship as the World

For the oldest FTA families, the ship is not a vehicle; it is the homeworld... The "Legacy Cairn" is the practice of interring crew members who die mid-voyage within the ship's own structure... The "Data-Psalm" is the ritual exchange of long, personal data-bursts and family histories with other Hauler-Clans...

V. The Driftways: The Unwritten Maps

The FTA's greatest strategic advantage over Helios and the Mandate is its mastery of secret, un-charted space... To betray the location of a Driftway is the ultimate crime within the Alliance, punishable by being permanently blacklisted from the Trust Ledger.
