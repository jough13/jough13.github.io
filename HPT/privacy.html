Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Health Physics Toolbox mobile application (the "App") developed by Joseph Donakowski.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The App is designed to operate as a strictly offline utility for health physics calculations. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, usage statistics, or user-generated content.

The App does not require an internet connection to function, and it has no mechanism to send data to external servers.

2. Local Device Storage

The App utilizes your device's local storage (e.g., LocalStorage) solely for the purpose of improving user experience. This includes:

  • Saving your interface preferences (e.g., Light Mode vs. Dark Mode).
  • Temporarily persisting your most recent calculation inputs (e.g., isotope selection or decay parameters) so you do not lose work if you close the App.

This data is stored entirely on your device. It is not accessible by the developer or any third parties and is removed if you clear the App's data or uninstall the App.
