Eldoria

Objective

The goal is to destroy your opponent's Stronghold. To do this, you must first break all three of their Provinces.

The Board

  • Provinces & Stronghold: Each player has 3 Provinces and 1 Stronghold. Each has a defense value (🛡️).
  • Hidden Cards: At the start of the game, a card from your deck is placed face-down under each Province. When a Province is broken, you draw that card.

Your Turn

  1. You gain 1 Max Mana (up to 10). Your mana is refilled.
  2. You draw one card.
  3. You can play cards from your hand by spending mana.
  4. Your creatures can attack. Each creature can only attack once per turn.

Attacking

To attack, click one of your ready creatures, then click a valid target (glowing red). You can attack:

  • Enemy Provinces: If your creature's attack is greater than or equal to the Province's defense, it breaks!
  • The Enemy Stronghold: Can only be attacked after all three enemy Provinces are broken.
  • Enemy Creatures: Creatures deal damage to each other equal to their attack power. Defeated creatures are sent to the discard pile.
