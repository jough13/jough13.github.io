How to Play Lexicon

Objective

Score as many points as you can by creating words (Level 1) or sentences (Level 2). Your high score is saved automatically!

Core Gameplay

  • Select a card from your hand to highlight it.
  • Click a Workshop Stack to place the selected card.
  • Point values, like in Scrabble, are shown on each letter card.

Level 1: Spell-a-Thon

  • Goal: Spell valid words on the stacks.
  • Scoring: When you've spelled a word, click the stack again (with no card selected) to score it.
  • Secret Word: Each game has a secret 4-letter word! The four stacks correspond to the letter positions. When you place the first letter on any stack, you'll get a clue:
    • Blue Border: Right letter in the right spot!
    • Green Border: Right letter, wrong spot.
  • Solve the secret word for a big point bonus! A new secret word will then be chosen.

Level 2: Sentence Smith

  • Goal: Create grammatically correct sentences.
  • Scoring: Sentences are scored all at once after you place the final punctuation card (e.g., a period).

Special Actions

  • Redraw Hand (-1 pt): Discards your hand permanently!
  • Clear Stack (-2 pts): Stuck on a word? **Double-click** a stack to clear it.
  • Rewrite Tile (*): Play the special * tile on a stack to replace its last letter with any letter you choose.
  • Buy Cards (escalating cost): Need more cards? Spend points to add 5 cards from your discard pile back into the deck.
